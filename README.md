# Apparel E-Commerce Operations & Sustainability Analysis

## 📊 Business Problem & Objective
This project addresses a critical operational bottleneck: a **37.43% clothing return rate** that severely impacts profitability due to reverse logistics costs. The objective was to build an end-to-end business intelligence solution to isolate why items are being returned, identify high-risk customer segments, and provide data-driven supply chain recommendations.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Power BI Desktop:** Data modeling, DAX measure engineering, and interactive dashboard design.
* **Power Query:** Data extraction, clearing column-loading errors, and schema transformations.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Engineered custom business logic for dynamic rate calculations.

## 🔍 Key Insights Uncovered
* **Top Operational Bottleneck:** **Defective items** represent the single largest driver of returns (179 total returns), pointing to upstream manufacturing or fabric quality control issues.
* **Fulfillment Friction:** "Changed Mind" (166) and "Wrong Item" (165) are virtually tied as the second-highest drivers, indicating warehouse picking mistakes and online product imagery mismatches.
* **Demographic Target:** **Female consumers account for 53.86% of total returns**, signaling a need to review product sizing and specifications for women's apparel.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
1. **Supplier Quality Audit:** Isolate specific high-defect product IDs to hold textile workshops/vendors accountable.
2. **Warehouse Quality Control:** Implement a double-verification protocol during order packaging to eliminate "Wrong Item" shipments.
3. **Enhanced Digital Catalog:** Update online sizing charts and product photos on sales channels to align accurately with physical inventory.
